NCC - Farmhouse

The project, assigned by the National Commission, Canada, is to design a sustainable farmhouse for a family of four. The site lies within the Ottawa's greenbelt area and is surrounded by an already existing barn, which inspires the minimalist concept of the facade

The house is pre-fabricated outside; assembled on site. The choice of materials used to build the farmhouse is sustainable and compliment the facade of the barn.
